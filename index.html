Biography

He is an Information Systems Engineer by the National University of Technology (UTN-FRCU), Argentina.

He is currently on the PhD Program on Computer Science of the National University of La Plata (UNLP), Argentina.

He works as researcher in the Information Systems Research Group (GISI) in National University of Lanús (UNLa).

The subject of his thesis is the Definition of Knowledge Discovery Processes for Spatial Information.
